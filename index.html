Material & Construction

Every Detail
Deliberate

The MarkNDye hoodie isn't built to look technical. It's built to be technical — and let the wearer discover that over time.

  • Fabric: 400 GSM heavyweight cotton fleece, double-faced
  • Hood: Integrated balaclava attachment with rear neckline anchor
  • Pockets: Hidden back-neck, dual side zip, hidden slant, joint kangaroo
  • Cuffs: Ribbed with set-in thumb holes (Pullover), plain ribbed (Zip-Up)
  • Hem: Ribbed with pocket bag stitched to hem for stability
  • Hardware: YKK® zippers, brushed gold finish
  • Dye: Hand-applied reactive dye, process varies per batch
FAQ
The balaclava attaches via a concealed snap at the interior rear neckline of the hood. Pull the hood down and away, align the balaclava's attachment point, and press firm. It detaches cleanly without leaving any external marks on the hoodie.
Hand-dyed pieces do develop a patina with wash and wear — we consider this a feature, not a flaw. Wash cold, inside out, with like colours on a gentle cycle. The character that develops is yours and yours alone.
These run true to size with an intentional oversized silhouette. If you prefer a more fitted look, size down. The 400 GSM fabric has minimal stretch, so allow for the structured drape in your choice.
We ship pan-India in 3–5 business days. Free shipping on orders above ₹1,999. Returns accepted within 30 days if unworn and unwashed — hand-dyed pieces cannot be exchanged for identical items, as no two are the same.
